Vote For Jerry B. Mercado

Jerry B. Mercado is the current President and Founder of New Brunswick Little League. Coaching since 2006, he coaches Baseball, Basketball, and Soccer. He saw the need for New Brunswick children to compete with other neighboring towns so they can improve their skills and feel a sense of accomplishment. He is also a father of three children all born in New Brunswick with one being hearing impaired. He has to be consistently advocating for his children to make sure they get the best education available to them. He now wishes to use his skills to advocate for all the children of New Brunswick.He graduated from Rutgers University with a degree in Industrial Engineering and a minor in Psychology. During his tenure at Rutgers University he was a student leader and organized activities and events for the Rutgers Community.

Priorities

  • Bring my experiences and skills as a Parent, Engineer, and leader of the community to the Board of Education.
  • Improve participation of parents and community at all schools
  • Ensure all students feel safe and secure at all schools
  • Increase the graduation rate of High School Students
  • Implement transparency in every school board process
  • Create fiscal accountability
  • Televise all School Board Meetings to have an informed community
