Who I Am

Jerry B. Mercado

Board of Education Candidate

Jerry B. Mercado was the current President and Founder of New Brunswick Little League for 5 years. Coaching since 2006, he coached Baseball, Basketball, and Soccer. He played Little League as a child growing up in Newark and wanted to give his son and all the children in New Brunswick the same opportunity he had compete with other neighboring towns This level of completion can improve their baseball skills and make them feel a sense of accomplishment. He is also a father of three children all born in New Brunswick with one being hearing impaired. He has to be consistently advocating for his children to make sure they get the best education available to them. He now wishes to use his skills to advocate for all the children of New Brunswick. Two of them graduated from the New Brunswick Health Science Technology High School and one is currently a student. He graduated from Rutgers University with a degree in Industrial Engineering and a minor in Psychology. During his tenure at Rutgers University he was a student leader and organized activities and events for the Rutgers Community. One year he was awarded Hall President of the Year for one of his programs that brought the Rutgers Community including faculty and custodians together with music.

My Plans

Improve participation of parents and community at all schools.

Ensure all students feel safe and secure at all schools.

Increase the graduation rate of High School Students.

Implement transparency in every school board process.

Create fiscal accountability.

Televise all School Board Meetings to have an informed community
